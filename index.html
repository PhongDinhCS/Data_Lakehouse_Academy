The Medallion Framework

The industry standard for organizing data quality and reliability in a Lakehouse environment.

Bronze: Ingestion

The Raw zone where source data lands. Focus on data preservation, lineage, and high-frequency ingestion.

LAB: Databricks Auto Loader

Silver: Transformation

Cleaning, filtering, and joining. Focus on schema enforcement, data quality rules, and deduplication.

LAB: Spark SQL & Python

Gold: Business Ready

Highly aggregated and project-specific. Focus on dimensional modeling (Star Schema) for final consumption.

LAB: Databricks SQL Warehouse
